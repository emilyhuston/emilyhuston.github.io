Writing

I'm an editor, writer, and photographer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I write about things that matter to me so they matter to you, too.

As the editor in chief for City College of San Francisco's award-winning magazine Etc, I've overseen the production of three print issues: from brainstorming story ideas to editing structure to designing layout, all culminating with delivering the final copy to the printer's desk.

Along the way, I've reported on food insecurity at City College, an Inner Sunset community garden that grows urban farmers, and the cultural evolution of gender prounouns.

  • HUNGRY MINDS: Pilot program addresses food insecurity among students
  • A WELL-STOCKED PANRY: City's College's new Rams Market provides free food for students
  • CITY COLLEGE ROOTS: How a hill of sand transformed into a flowering oasis
  • ITS NAME IS JOHN: Pronouns adapt to reflect the full gender rainbow
