Writing

I'm an editor, writer, and photographer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I write about things that matter to me so they matter to you, too.

As the editor in chief for City College of San Francisco's magazine Etc, I oversaw the production of three print issues: from brainstorming story ideas to editing structure to designing layout, all culminating with delivering the final copy to the printer's desk. Under my direction, Etc won SF Press Club's "Best College Magazine" award three years in a row.
